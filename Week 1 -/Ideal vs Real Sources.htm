Ideal vs Real Sources

Monday, July 30, 2018

12:28 PM

    • Ideal voltage and current sources can generate/dissipate infinite power
    • Real voltage and current sources have a limit to their power capabilities

     

    Ideal voltage sources have no internal resistance (series)

    Real voltage sources have a very low (non-zero) internal resistance

     

    Ideal current sources have infinite internal resistance (parallel)

    Real current sources have a very-high but non-infinite internal resistance
