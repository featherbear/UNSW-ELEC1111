Conductance

  • Conductance is the ability of an element to conduct electric current
  • It is the reciprocal of resistance
  • Conductance G is measured in mho (Ʊ) or siemens (S).
    • Siemens are amperes per volt

 

 

Linear vs Non-Linear Resistance

 

Linear

Most resistance have linear resistance, and obey Ohm's law

 

Non-Linear

Some circuit elements do not obey Ohm's Law, and have non-linear resistance, whose voltage varies with current
